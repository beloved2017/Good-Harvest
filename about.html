OUR FAQ

Freequently Ask Questions

01.What types of solar systems do you offer, and are they suitable for both homes and businesses?

We offer complete solar systems, including high-quality panels and batteries, suitable for both residential and commercial use. Our systems are designed to provide reliable, cost-effective power solutions..

02.What are the differences between the 3kg and 6kg gas cylinders?

The 3kg gas cylinder is more compact and ideal for light cooking needs, while the 6kg cylinder is perfect for more frequent use and larger households. Both are durable and easy to handle.

03.Are the Chinese fruits and vegetables fresh and available year-round?

Yes, we ensure that our Chinese fruits and vegetables are always fresh. We strive to keep them in stock year-round, providing high-quality produce for your culinary needs.

04.What is the benefit of using screeding cement for interior and exterior surfaces?

Screeding cement provides a smooth, even surface for walls and floors, enhancing the durability and finish of your construction project. It’s ideal for both interior and exterior applications.

05.Do you offer installation services for the kitchen sinks and wash hand basins?

Yes, we can connect you with professional installation services to ensure your wash hand basins and kitchen sinks are installed properly and efficiently..
